ODOO POS Advanced Bundle Product

Create Product combos with Odoo POS Advanced Bundle Product!

Odoo POS Advanced Bundle Product enables you to create product bun POS. Further, there are six categories (required, required optional, add-on, accessory item, optional, and radio). Each product bundle has a 'Product Bundle' tag to it. In addition, you can choose to show/hide the advanced bundle in POS.

How POS Product Bundles Increase Sales?

You sell products from your store, and there are few products that customers buy more often, while there are some products that they buy occasionally. So to increase sales, you can create a bundle with both such products to ensure sales of both products. Moreover, the bundle allows you to add diverse complementary product bundles to be sold together.

Reduce the Marketing Cost with POS Advanced Bundle Product

One of the advantages of selling products in the bundle is that you don't have to advertise/market your product individually. You can offer the product bundle as a whole. Further, when you sell bundled products, you add complementary products any various add-ons.

Most importantly, it enhances efficiency by reducing the marketing cost, as the products are clubbed together in a bundle.

Add Various Type of Products in Bundle

POS Advanced Bundle product enables you to add six different categories of products. Also, these options include options such as required, required optional, add-on, accessory item, optional, and radio. All these categories have different options for customers to choose from them. Most importantly, some of these categories allow you to add only one compulsory product, while some other enables you to offer multiple options.

Detailed Features List

Create Advanced Product Bundle in Odoo POS

  • Enables you to create fully customizable product bundles in Odoo POS.
  • Add multiple additional options in a single Odoo product Bundle.
  • Users and customers can choose from available options and customize the bundle as per their preferences or need.

Advanced Selection Option To Choose From

  • You can add numerous types of input to the product configuration.
  • Includes input types such as required, required optional, add-on, accessory item, optional, and radio.
  • Input types give various selection options to customers.
  • Customization options are displayed on the POS screen.

Records Bundle Product Details for Orders in the Odoo Backend

  • Admin can view the details of items chosen by the customer from the bundle in the order line.

Product Bundle Details on the POS Receipt

  • Users and customers can view the details of the product bundle in the POS receipt.

Separate Category to Show Advanced Bundle Products in POS

  • Creates a separate category to show bundles in POS.
  • Each POS bundle has the tag 'Product Bundle' on it.

Product Bundle Details in the Odoo POS

  • Customers can see and edit product bundles in the POS to choose the desired product
  • Includes input types such as required, required optional, add-on, accessory item, optional, and radio.
  • Certain items might not be editable depending upon their category.
