Odoo Website Advanced Bundle Product

Quick Product Bundle Builder For Odoo

The module facilitates you to create fully customizable product bundles for Odoo website. Each product in the advanced bundle has a separate attribute such as required, optional, add-on, etc. The customer can customize the bundle using these options while purchasing it from the website.

  • Facilitates you to choose from the available options in a Odoo Bundle Product to make your own product pack.
  • Engage Website Customers With Multiple Choices For Product Bundle.
Let Customers Pick their Own Mix From A Odoo Product Bundle
  • Create & Publish Bundle Products on Odoo Website.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Advanced Bundle Product

Create Customizable Product Bundles In Odoo

  • Allows you to create totally customizable Product bundle in Odoo.
  • Add multiple additional options in a single Odoo product Bundle.
  • The User or customer can choose from these multiple options and customize the bundle.

Advanced Selection Options included

  • A Number of input types are added to the product configuration.
  • Includes Input types such as radio button, checkbox, required type, optional type, etc.
  • Use these input types to give various selection options to customers.
  • The customization options are displayed on the product page of a product bundle.

Works for Both - Odoo Website & Backend

  • The features works on website as well as Odoo backend.
  • Use bundle products to place order in backend.
  • Publish bundle products on odoo website for customers.

Records Bundle Product Details for Orders in backend

  • The Sales orderline shows details of items bought from the product bundle by customer.
  • Similarly, website orders shows which products were bought from the product bundle.

Separate Menu For Advanced/Customizable Bundle Products in Odoo

  • Created separate Menu to show list of Product Bundles to Odoo User.

Multi-Website Compatible

  • The module is made to be compatible with multiple Odoo website.

Product Bundle Details in Website Cart

  • The customer can see and edit the details of items in the cart on website.
  • (Certain item may not be editable depending upon constraints of the bundle.)

Product Bundle Details in Customer’s Website Portal

  • The customer can check the details of ordered product bundle in his/her website account.
  • Input Types
  • Configuration
  • Website View

Required Items With main product

The customer needs to purchase all the items under Required with the main product on Website.

Required Optional

The customer can needs to purchase either all the items or none under this category.

Add-On and Accessories Items

Other optional Category are Accessories and Add-on items. The customer can change the quantities of items under this.

Optional Items

Optional Items allow for one, more than one or none of the items from the list. The quantity of the items cannot be changed under Optional list.
